Prototype Notes · For David

A page that closes the proof gap.

This is what the LND /portfolio page could look like — real homes, real addresses, real neighborhoods, no stock photography. The five properties below are pulled from your public Zillow listings; specs are confirmed where Triad MLS data was public and left as em-dashes where they need to come from your records.

The biggest unlock isn't the layout — it's the photography. Whatever photographer shot these homes for the MLS already has the originals in higher resolution than what's on Zillow. Pull those, drop them into the placeholders, and this page is ready to go live.

01.Photos

Three to four photos per home is enough — exterior hero, kitchen, living, primary suite. Pull from listing photographer's originals if available; iPhone shots from final walkthroughs work too.

02.Confirm specs

Three of the five homes have em-dashes where beds/baths/sqft need to be filled in. These are public on the MLS and easy to pull from your own records — five minutes of work.

03.Credit the agent

Listing each home with the buyer's agent's brokerage builds trust and reciprocity. Agents who see themselves credited become referral partners.
